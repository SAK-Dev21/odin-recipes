Lamb Curry

Description

This recipe is a classic South Asian recipe! Made up of lamb, several sensational and spicy seasonings, and vegetables, this is sure to tingle your tastebuds and relive your digestion system!

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1-2 Onions
  • 2 Teaspoons of Ginger.
  • 2 Teaspoons of Garlic.

Meat

  • Lamb meat

Other

  • Olive oil

Seasoning

  • Cinnamon powder or sticks
  • Bay leaves
  • Cardamom pods
  • Cumin seeds
  • 4-5 Tablespoons of Curry powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Cumin
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Turmeric
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Garam masala
  • 1-3 Teaspoons of Salt (Add accordingly based on taste as you use it)
