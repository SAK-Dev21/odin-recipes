Egg Fried Rice

Description

This recipe is a Chinese recipe composed of egg coated on rice and vegetables and fried. It's quick, easy and delicious to make!

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1 Green onion (Spring onion)
  • Frozen vegetables pack (or any vegetables of your choice)

Other

  • X1 Egg
  • Boiled rice
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sesame oil

Seasoning

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • White pepper

Sweeteners

  • 1 Teaspoon of sugar

Preparation steps

  1. Boil rice in advance
  2. Chop up the Green Onion and separate the white and green parts.

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the following:
    1. X1 Egg.
    2. 1-2 Teaspoons of Salt.
    3. Pepper.
    4. Boiled rice.
      • Break parts of the rice into smaller pieces to fry it easier.
  2. In a pan, add the following and cook on light heat:
    1. Olive oil.
    2. Green parts of the green onion.
    3. Egg mixture.
  3. Simmer (cook on low heat) for 30 seconds.
  4. Gently mix (scramble) the egg mixture until it resembles scrambled eggs.
  5. Add Frozen Vegetables and simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add the following and mix for 2-3 minutes on light-medium heat:
    1. Rice.
    2. Some Soy sauce.
    3. 1 Teaspoon of Sugar.
    4. Some White pepper.
    5. Some Oyster sauce.
    6. Some Sesame oil.
  7. Add in the Rest of the Green Onion (the white parts). Fry for 1-2 minutes.
  8. DONE!
