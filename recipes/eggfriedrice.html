Egg Fried Rice

Description

This recipe is a Chinese recipe composed of egg coated on rice and vegetables and fried. It's quick, easy and delicious to make!

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1 Green onion
  • Frozen vegetables pack (or any vegetables of your choice)

Other

  • X1 Egg
  • Boiled rice
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sesame oil

Seasoning

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • White pepper

Sweeteners

  • 1 Teaspoon of sugar
