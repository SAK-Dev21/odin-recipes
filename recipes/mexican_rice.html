Mexican Rice

Description

This recipe is a dish straight from Mexico! Perfect for vegetarians, this dish is comprised of rice and vegetables.

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1 Onion
  • X1 Jalapeno Pepper
  • X2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 of a Tomato
  • Optional: Add more vegetables

Other

  • Olive oil
  • Rice

Seasoning

  • 1 Teaspoon of Paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon of Turmeric
  • 1 Teaspoon of Black Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • X1 cup of Chicken stock or X1 Meat cube
  • Tomato paste
