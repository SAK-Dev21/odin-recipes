Mexican Rice

Description

This recipe is a dish straight from Mexico! Perfect for vegetarians, this dish is comprised of rice and vegetables.

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1 Onion
  • X1 Jalapeno Pepper
  • X2-3 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 of a Tomato
  • Optional: Add more vegetables

Other

  • Olive oil
  • Rice
  • X1 cup of Chicken stock or X1 Meat cube
  • Tomato paste

Seasoning

  • 1 Teaspoon of Paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon of Turmeric
  • 1 Teaspoon of Black Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt

Preparation steps

  1. Wash rice (if you haven't done so already).
  2. Lay out cooking equipment.
  3. Chop up any vegetables.

Steps

  1. Heat up pan, then add Olive oil at light heat.
  2. Add onions. Allow for them to look golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and mix.
  4. Add seasoning (listed above).
  5. Add rice and mix.
  6. Add Jalapeno pepper (and any optional additional vegetables) and mix.
  7. Add Tomato paste and mix.
  8. Add meat stock.
    • Note: If using a stock cube, dissolve it in hot water beforehand.
  9. Cover the pan with a lid and cook on low to medium heat for about 20-30 minutes.
    • Check occasionally to ensure the rice isnâ€™t sticking or drying out. Add a splash of water if necessary.
  10. Once the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed, remove it from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  11. DONE! Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
