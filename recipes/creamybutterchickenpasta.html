Creamy Butter Chicken Pasta

Description

Want to hibernate in the toilet! Try Creamy Butter Chicken Pasta! Made up of spices and seasoning mixed with chicken and pasta, with some creaminess on top!

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1 Onions
  • X1 Chopped Tomatoes OR Tomatoes actually chopped
  • X1 Garlic

Meat

  • Chicken breast

Other

  • Lemon juice / Squeezing 2 lemons
  • Olive oil
  • Low Fat Yogurt (Or Yogurt in general)
  • Pasta

Seasoning

  • 1 Teaspoon of Paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon of Turmeric
  • 1 Teaspoon of Black pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon of Garam masala
  • 1 Teaspoon of Chilli powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
