Keema curry

Description

This recipe is another classic South Asian recipe! Made up of minced meat, several sensational and spicy seasonings, and vegetables, this is sure to tingle your tastebuds and relive your digestion system! You can even have this with pasta, rice or any bread of your choosing!

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1-2 Onions
  • 1 Teaspoon of Ginger.
  • 1 Teaspoon of Garlic.
  • 1/2 of a Tomato

Meat

  • Minced meat

Other

  • Olive oil
  • Potatoes

Seasoning

  • Some Cinnamon powder or sticks
  • A few Bay leaves
  • A few Cardamom pods
  • Some Cumin seeds
  • 2-3 Tablespoons of Curry powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Cumin
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Turmeric
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Garam masala
  • 1-3 Teaspoons of Salt (Add accordingly based on taste as you use it)
