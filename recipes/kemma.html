Keema curry

Description

This recipe is another classic South Asian recipe! Made up of minced meat, several sensational and spicy seasonings, and vegetables, this is sure to tingle your tastebuds and relive your digestion system! You can even have this with pasta, rice or any bread of your choosing!

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • X1-2 Onions
  • 1 Teaspoon of Ginger.
  • 1 Teaspoon of Garlic.
  • 1-2 Tomatoes

Meat

  • Minced meat

Other

  • Olive oil
  • Potatoes

Seasoning

  • Some Cinnamon powder or sticks
  • A few Bay leaves
  • A few Cardamom pods
  • Some Cumin seeds
  • 2-3 Tablespoons of Curry powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Cumin
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Turmeric
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Garam masala
  • 1-3 Teaspoons of Salt (Add accordingly based on taste as you use it)

Preparation steps

  1. Chop up onions and prepare any other vegetable or carbohydrate to be used.
  2. Prepare seasoning to be left out.
  3. Prepare cooking equipment.
  4. Heat up water on a pan or with a kettle.

Steps

    Part 1

  1. Heat up pan.
  2. Add oil and wait until oil bubbles (test by putting a big spoon on the pan's surface).
  3. Add onions, garlic and ginger. Mix well.
  4. Add ingredients in Seasoning. Mix well.
  5. Add tomatoes and mix well.
  6. Add hot water to the mixture until the ingredients are barely submerged by the water (think of being in a bath, there is water coming up to you but you aren't drowning). Cook on low heat for 10 minutes.

    7. Part 2

  7. After 10 minutes have passed, add minced meat to the pan and mix well.
  8. Add Potatoes.
  9. Add water if needed.
    • The mixture should look like it is barely sticking out of the water (remember the bath analogy).
  10. Cook on low heat for 40-50 minutes and put a lid on the pan.
    • During this time, every 20 minutes or so, check on the mixture from time to time and stir gently. Taste with a teaspoon to see if you should add more salt.
  11. Once ready, turn off the heat and let the pan cool for a few minutes.
  12. DONE!
